Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Basic and Premium?

Basic ($3/month) includes all core features but shows native ads. Premium ($5/month) removes all ads for a completely ad-free experience.

What is included in the free trial?

Your 1-month free trial gives you complete access to all the features of the plan you choose. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes will be reflected in the next billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure your financial data is always safe and private.
